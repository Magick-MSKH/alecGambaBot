# :slot_machine: **Alec Stream Gamba Bot – User Manual** :slot_machine:

This is the official **"Alec Stream Gamba Bot"** user manual! 
You can now earn channel points just by hanging out, chatting, and supporting the stream.
Use your points to place bets and climb the all-time channel leaderboard.


## :moneybag: **How to Earn Points** :moneybag:

Points are tracked automatically -- your balance will never reset between streams! (Unless something catastrophic happens...)
Here are the ways that channel points can be earned:

* **New Users:** New users get 1000 points to start. Simply send a chat message while the bot is active to register!
* **Active Chatting:** Earn 10 points per chat message (30 second cooldown)
* **Passive Watching:** Earn 50 points every 5 minutes automatically if you have typed in chat within the last 15 minutes.
* **Channel Member Bonus:** Members of the channel receive a 1.2x point multiplier on all chatting rewards!
* **Super Chats:** Earn 250 points per $1 donated via Super Chat. (Example: $5 = 1250 points)
* **Gifted Memberships:** Earn 1500 points per Gifted Membership!
* **Member Milestones:** When you use your free monthly member milestone chat, you receive 1000 points, plus 250 per month subbed!
* **Bonus Check-in:** Once per stream, you can claim a free 500 points bonus! Check the Viewer Commands below for more info!


## :joystick: **Public Viewer Commands** :joystick:

Type these directly into the YouTube livestream chat to check statistics!

* **!balance**           Check your current point total.  (`!points` &  `!cash` also work)
* **!stats**                 View your profile stats (Current points, Total Bets Placed, Wins, and Losses)
* **!leaderboard**  Displays the top 5 richest players currently in the channel. (`!top` also works)
* **!record**             Displays the highest point peak ever achieved by a single player. (`!peak` also works)
* **!help**                  Displays a quick summary of commands. (`!commands` also works)
* **!check_in**         Claim your once-per-stream bonus points! (`!daily` & `!bonus` also work)


## :game_die: **How to Play: Live Betting** (!gamba) :game_die:

When a betting pool is opened by an admin, you can gamba your points on the outcome of said pool.
After a betting pool is opened, place a bet by using `!gamba [amount] [choice]`
**Example:** `!gamba 250 yes`
**NEW!!** Bet **ALL** your points with `!gamba all [choice]` (`all-in`, `max` & `maxbet` also work)
### Some things to keep in mind:
* Your bet [amount] must be greater than 0
* You cannot change your vote or place another bet once your bet is locked in.
* Choosing an option outside of the scope of choices will not work.

**Payout:** All winning players receive a 2x payout on their original bet amount. If you lose, your points are taken by the house.
**Refund Safety**: If unforeseen circumstances prevent the gamba from being resolved fairly, an admin can refund all player's points instantly by clearing the betting pool.
