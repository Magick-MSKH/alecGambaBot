💰 How to Earn Points

Points are tracked automatically -- your balance will never reset between streams! (Unless something catastrophic happens...)
Here are the ways that channel points can be earned:

* Active Chatting: Earn 10 points per chat message (30 second cooldown)
* Passive Watching: Earn 50 points every 5 minutes automatically if you have typed in chat within the last 15 minutes.
* Channel Member Bonus: Members of the channel receive a 1.2x point multiplier on all chatting rewards!
* Super Chats: Earn 250 points per $1 donated via Super Chat. (Example: $5 = 1250 points)
* Gifted Memberships: Earn 1500 points per Gifted Membership!
* Member Milestones: When you use your free monthly member milestone chat, you receive 1000 points, plus 250 per month subbed!


🕹️ Public Viewer Commands

Type these directly into the YouTube livestream chat to check statistics!

* !balance      Check your current point total
* !stats        View your profile stats (Current points, Total Bets Placed, Wins, and Losses)
* !leaderboard  Displays the top 5 richest players currently in the channel
* !record       Displays the highest point peak ever achieved by a single player
* !help         Diplays a quick summary of commands


🎲 How to Play: Live Betting (!gamba)

When a betting pool is opened by an admin, you can gamba your points on the outcome of said pool.
After a betting pool is opened, place a bet by using !gamba [amount] [vote_choice].
Example: !gamba 250 yes
Some things to keep in mind:
1. Your bet [amount] must be greater than 0
2. You cannot change your vote or place another bet once your bet is locked in.
3. Choosing an option outside of the scope of choices will not work.

Payout: All winning players receive a 2x payout on their original bet amount. If you lose, your points are taken by the house.
Refund Safety: If unforseen circumstances prevent the gamba from being resolved fairly, an admin can refund all player's points instantly by clearing the betting pool.
